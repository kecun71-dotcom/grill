# 15 Must-Try BBQ Side Dishes That Steal the Show

Great BBQ isn't just about the meatâ€”the sides make the meal complete. Here are 15 crowd-pleasing dishes to round out your next cookout.

## Classic Sides

### 1. Creamy Coleslaw

**Ingredients:**
- 1 medium cabbage, shredded
- 2 carrots, grated
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Salt and pepper

**Tip:** Make it a day ahead for best flavor.

### 2. Baked Beans

**Key ingredients:**
- Navy beans or canned beans
- Bacon
- Brown sugar
- Molasses
- Mustard
- Onion

**Grill method:** Bake in a cast iron pan on the grill for smoky flavor.

### 3. Potato Salad

**Variations:**
- Classic: Mayo, mustard, celery, eggs
- German: Warm, vinegar-based, bacon
- Loaded: Cheese, bacon, sour cream

### 4. Corn on the Cob

**Grilling options:**
- Direct heat (charred): 10-15 min, rotating
- In husk (steamed): 20-25 min
- Foil-wrapped (tender): 15-20 min

## Grilled Vegetables

### 5. Grilled Zucchini

Slice lengthwise, oil, season, grill 3-4 minutes per side.

### 6. Grilled Asparagus

Toss with olive oil, grill perpendicular to grates, 5-7 minutes.

### 7. Grilled Bell Peppers

Quarter and remove seeds, grill until charred, peel skin optional.

### 8. Grilled Portobello Mushrooms

Marinate, grill cap-side down first, serve as a side or burger.

## Bread & Starches

### 9. Garlic Bread on the Grill

Slice baguette, brush with garlic butter, grill 2-3 minutes per side.

### 10. Cornbread

Bake in a cast iron skillet on the grill for authentic flavor.

### 11. Mac and Cheese

**Upgrade tips:**
- Use smoked cheese
- Add breadcrumb topping
- Finish on the grill

### 12. Grilled Potato Wedges

Parboil, oil and season, grill until crispy (15-20 minutes).

## Fresh & Light

### 13. Cucumber Salad

- Sliced cucumbers
- Red onion
- Rice vinegar
- Sugar
- Dill

### 14. Watermelon Feta Salad

- Cubed watermelon
- Crumbled feta
- Fresh mint
- Balsamic glaze

### 15. Grilled Caesar Salad

Grill romaine halves for 2 minutes, top with Caesar dressing and parmesan.

## Side Dish Pairing Guide

| Main Dish | Best Sides |
|-----------|------------|
| Brisket | Coleslaw, beans, potato salad |
| Ribs | Cornbread, mac & cheese, beans |
| Burgers | Coleslaw, potato wedges, corn |
| Chicken | Grilled veggies, corn, cucumber salad |
| Seafood | Light salads, grilled asparagus |

## Make-Ahead Tips

**Day before:**
- Coleslaw (let flavors meld)
- Potato salad
- Baked beans base
- Cucumber salad

**Morning of:**
- Prep all vegetables
- Make dressings
- Parboil potatoes

**On the grill:**
- Corn
- Vegetables
- Garlic bread
