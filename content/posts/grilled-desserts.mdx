# Grilled Desserts: Sweet Endings from Your BBQ

## Why Grill Desserts?

- Caramelizes natural sugars
- Adds subtle smoky notes
- Creates impressive presentation
- Uses residual heat efficiently
- Unexpected and memorable

## Grilled Fruit Favorites

### 1. Grilled Peaches with Ice Cream

**Ingredients:**
- 4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted
- 2 tbsp melted butter
- 2 tbsp honey
- Vanilla ice cream
- Crushed amaretti cookies (optional)

**Method:**
1. Brush cut sides with butter
2. Grill cut-side down for 3-4 minutes
3. Flip and drizzle with honey
4. Grill 2 more minutes
5. Serve with ice cream

### 2. Caramelized Pineapple

**Ingredients:**
- 1 pineapple, cored and sliced into rings
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Coconut ice cream

**Method:**
1. Coat pineapple with sugar and cinnamon
2. Grill 3-4 minutes per side
3. Serve with coconut ice cream
4. Drizzle with rum (optional)

### 3. Grilled Watermelon

**Yes, really!** Grilling intensifies sweetness.

**Serving ideas:**
- With feta and mint
- With balsamic reduction
- With lime and chili powder

### 4. Grilled Banana Boats

**Ingredients:**
- Bananas (unpeeled)
- Chocolate chips
- Mini marshmallows
- Crushed graham crackers

**Method:**
1. Slit banana lengthwise (don't cut through bottom peel)
2. Stuff with chocolate and marshmallows
3. Wrap in foil
4. Grill 5-8 minutes
5. Top with graham cracker crumbs

## Grilled Pound Cake

Store-bought pound cake transforms on the grill.

**Steps:**
1. Slice pound cake 1-inch thick
2. Brush with melted butter
3. Grill 1-2 minutes per side
4. Top with grilled fruit and whipped cream

## Grilled S'mores

### Classic Method
- Toast marshmallows on skewers over flames
- Sandwich with chocolate and graham crackers

## Grilled Pizza Dessert

**Dough preparation:**
- Use store-bought pizza dough
- Stretch into small rounds
- Grill 2 minutes per side

**Topping ideas:**
| Style | Toppings |
|-------|----------|
| Nutella Pizza | Nutella, strawberries, powdered sugar |
| Apple Pie | Butter, cinnamon sugar, apple slices |
| Tropical | Cream cheese, grilled pineapple, coconut |

## Grilled Donuts

Yes, you can grill donuts!

**Method:**
1. Use store-bought glazed donuts
2. Brush with melted butter
3. Grill 30 seconds per side
4. The glaze caramelizes beautifully

**Serve with:**
- Ice cream
- Fresh berries
- Chocolate sauce

## Foil Packet Desserts

### Berry Crisp Packets

**Per packet:**
- 1 cup mixed berries
- 2 tbsp granola
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- Pinch of cinnamon

Seal in foil, grill 10-15 minutes.

### Apple Cinnamon Packets

**Per packet:**
- 1 apple, sliced
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of nutmeg

Seal in foil, grill 15-20 minutes.

## Grilling Tips for Desserts

### Temperature
- Use medium to medium-low heat
- Sweet items burn quickly
- Residual heat after main course is perfect

### Timing
- Fruit: 2-4 minutes per side
- Cake/bread: 1-2 minutes per side
- Foil packets: 10-20 minutes

### Clean Grill
- Sweet items stick more
- Oil grates well
- Use a clean section of the grill
