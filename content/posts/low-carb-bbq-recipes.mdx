# Keto & Low-Carb BBQ: Delicious Recipes Without the Guilt

BBQ and low-carb eating go together perfectly—most grilled meats are naturally keto-friendly. Here's how to enjoy a full BBQ spread while staying on track.

## Naturally Low-Carb BBQ Foods

### Zero-Carb Options
- Beef steaks
- Pork chops
- Lamb chops
- Chicken thighs (skin-on)
- Brisket
- Ribs (without sauce)
- Fish and seafood

### Low-Carb Options (< 5g per serving)
- Chicken wings
- Sausages (check labels)
- Bacon-wrapped anything
- Grilled vegetables

## Low-Carb BBQ Sauce

Traditional BBQ sauce is loaded with sugar. Try this keto version:

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup tomato sauce (no sugar added)
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar-free ketchup
- 1 tbsp liquid smoke
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp cayenne
- Sweetener to taste (erythritol, monk fruit)

**Net carbs:** ~2g per 2 tbsp serving

## Keto Dry Rub

Skip the sugar, keep the flavor:

**Ingredients:**
- 2 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cayenne

## Low-Carb Side Dishes

### 1. Cauliflower "Potato" Salad
Replace potatoes with roasted cauliflower.
**Net carbs:** ~4g per serving

### 2. Creamy Coleslaw (Keto)
Use mayo and a touch of apple cider vinegar—skip the sugar.
**Net carbs:** ~3g per serving

### 3. Grilled Zucchini
Slice thick, season, grill.
**Net carbs:** ~2g per serving

### 4. Bacon-Wrapped Asparagus
Wrap asparagus bundles in bacon, grill until crispy.
**Net carbs:** ~2g per serving

### 5. Caprese Salad
Fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil, olive oil.
**Net carbs:** ~4g per serving

### 6. Stuffed Mushrooms
Fill with cream cheese, bacon, and herbs.
**Net carbs:** ~2g per serving

## Bunless Burger Options

### Lettuce Wraps
Butter lettuce or iceberg works great.

### Portobello Bun
Grilled portobello caps as top and bottom.

### Cheese Shell
Crispy cheese "buns" made from melted cheddar.

## Tips for Staying Low-Carb at BBQs

### At Home
- Prep keto-friendly sauces in advance
- Have plenty of low-carb sides
- Skip the bread basket

### As a Guest
- Eat before if unsure about options
- Bring a low-carb dish to share
- Focus on grilled meats and salads
- Skip sauces or ask what's in them
